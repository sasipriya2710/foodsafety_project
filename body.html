WELCOME TO FOOD SAFETY PAGE!


Hey there,fellow foodies! Let's talk about something really important:food safety!


You know,food safety is like being a superhero for your stomach.You get to save the day by keeping the bad guys(like bacteria and gems) from ruining your meal!


So,here are some super-simple tips to keep your food safe:

  1. Wash those hands! before and after handling food,give those hands a good scrub with soap and water.
  2. Separate,separate!keep raw meat,poultry and seafood separate from ready-to-eat foods.No cross-combination,please!
  3. Cook it right! make sure your food is cooked to a safe internal temperature.Use a food thermometer to check!
  4. Chill,baby,chill! Refrigerate perishable foods promptly and keep your fridge at 40 degree or below
  5. Defrost safety! Thaw frozen foods in the fridge,cold water, or the microwave.never thaw at room temperature!

by following these easy-peasy tips,you'll be a food safety superhero in no time!
Remember, food safety is everyone's responsibility.Let's all do our part to keep our food safe and our tummies happy!.
Happy cooking and stay safe!
